Automated Lead Management Engine

Project Summary

Delivered a next-generation, data-driven lead management and opportunity intelligence framework tailored for high-velocity automotive sales environments. The solution was built to replace reactive, siloed CRM workflows with a proactive, real-time system focused on optimizing conversion efficiency, accountability, and responsiveness.

The framework was implemented through an immersive Power BI experience, featuring custom HTML-animated visuals and integrated automation. It unified CRM, marketing, and sales data streams to create a single source of intelligence for lead tracking, prioritization, and escalation.

🔧 Core Capabilities

  • 🕒 Stage-Level Time-to-Action (TAT) Analytics: Calculated time spent per funnel stage (Created → Experiencing → Deciding → Converted) with SLA benchmarking and delay detection.
  • 📊 Dynamic Lead Scoring & Tiering: Real-time scoring model based on channel conversion history, volume contribution, stage proximity, and behavioral signals; output tiers: Hot, Warm, Cold.
  • ⚖️ Load Factor & KPI Relaxation: Load-aware performance adjustments using sigmoid decay logic, ensuring fair SLA targets during high sales volume periods.
  • ⏳ 90th Percentile Expiry Modeling: Classified leads as "Active" or "Expired" based on empirical thresholds per stage, improving forecast accuracy and pipeline cleanliness.
  • ⚙️ Real-Time Alerts & Automation: Power Automate-driven escalations triggered by SLA breaches, inactivity, or Tier 1 lead stagnation—automatically sent to mapped stakeholders.

📈 Dashboard & Visualization Highlights

  • 🎨 Custom HTML & DAX Animations: Interactive bubble charts, stage dials, tachometers, and funnel movement visuals for intuitive, story-based insights.
  • 🔄 Automated Data Refresh: Seamless ETL pipeline with daily ingestion from CRM, finance, and marketing platforms—supporting near real-time dashboards.
  • 🎯 KPI-Centric Architecture: Dashboards structured around key business metrics such as lead TAT, delay rate, conversion velocity, and ROI by source/channel.
  • 🧠 AI-Style Narrative Insights: Embedded logic to generate human-like summaries that interpret movements, identify anomalies, and suggest corrective actions.
  • 👥 Multi-Level Views: Executive summary views with drilldowns by dealer, channel, nameplate, market, and sales stage—ensuring cross-functional clarity.

🚀 Strategic Outcome

The result was a robust, enterprise-grade operating system for sales performance—enabling C-suite visibility, faster decision-making, cleaner forecasting, and improved pipeline accountability across regional sales networks. It transitioned the sales process from passive tracking to precision opportunity execution.
